About us

Built on Hard Work and Clear Standards

Locally owned since 2023, 920 Window Pros LLC serves Wisconsin homeowners from Milwaukee to Green Bay with reliable, professional window cleaning and pressure washing services. We're committed to quality work and showing up when we say we will.

Our Mission

We show up on time, we do the job right, and we leave your home looking better than we found it—every visit, every window, every time.

Our Vision

To be the residential window and exterior-care team Wisconsin homeowners recommend first: known for honest quotes, careful crews, and results you notice the moment you walk through the door.

What Sets Us Apart

Attention to Detail

Every window, screen, and surface gets the thorough treatment it deserves. We don't cut corners or rush through jobs.

Always Insured

Fully licensed and insured for your peace of mind. Your property is protected, and our work is guaranteed.

Friendly Service

We treat your home with respect and communicate clearly throughout the process. No surprises, just results.

Meet the Team

Founded by Wisconsin natives who understand what it means to take care of your neighbors' homes. We started 920 Window Pros because we saw too many fly-by-night services that didn't stand behind their work.

Today, we're proud to serve communities throughout southeastern Wisconsin with the same standards we'd want for our own families.

Wisconsin Proud

As a Wisconsin-based small business, we're invested in our communities. When you choose 920 Window Pros, you're supporting local jobs and working with people who understand the unique challenges of maintaining homes in our climate.
